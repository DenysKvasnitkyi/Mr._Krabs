Mr. Krabs

I'm Eugene Harold Krabs, and I am owner and manager of the most popular restaurant in Bikini Bottom - Krusty Krab. In our food established I act as the boss, and I'm responsible for controlling the actions of my wards. And also - I like money! You can see that by watching the following video.

Review

My love to money is beyond measure, I like to earn it, but I don't like to spend it. It's hard to leave with such big love to money, it's very difficult even to buy a single ice cream. That's all me and there is nothing to do with it. You have to accept it about me.

It was general information about me. If you want to know more about me, you can read many interesting details below.

Parents

There are my dad Victor Krabs and my mom Betsy Krabs. They are the ones who raised me and taught me how to save money, which greatly affected my future life.

Father is an extremely elderly crab, over 90 years old, with a lightened pink shell and wrinkles. He has a large bushy gray mustache, and wears a turtleneck sweater with light blue edges and a pink and light blue diamond pattern, a white shirt, and purple pants. He also speaks in a pirate accent much like my grandpa.

Betsy Krabs is a nice caring mother, as she loves me and is being nice to everyone she meet. She has wrinkly light red skin, wears a purple dress and large blue glasses, and has a small patch of fluffy white hair.

Daughter

Pearl is my daughter, she is a young whale who lives in a Hollow anchor with me. Pearl is happy, naive, well-meaning, and high-spirited. She wants nothing more than to fit in with the other residents of Bikini Bottom, but this is impossible to do because she is literally the biggest teenage girl in the ocean. She is skilled in math, which is why I would like it if she became a bookkeeper for my restaurant. However, she is still young and has not matured yet, so she spends most of her time and money at the mall. Aside from being skilled in math, Pearl's talents include cheerleading, playing the saxophone, and playing the piano. She often keeps a pair of red or pink cheerleading pom-poms with her. Being a whale, she shakes the ground whenever she tries to perform a cheerleading act and endangers whoever is in the same building as her. Pearl has been on the high school cheer squad for at least two years. I think she is the "best little cheerleader in the seven seas".

Pearl has gray skin and is very tall compared to the other people. She has blonde hair that she ties in a ponytail with a pink scrunchie. There is a red heart shape on the bottom of her nose that turns into lips when she kisses. She has solid blue, oval-shaped eyes and six eyelashes on each eye. Pearl has five ventral pleats on her neck, usually drawn as five lines with the centered one longer than the rest.

Enemies

This is my the worst enemy Plankton, who, with his computer girlfriend Karen own a restaurant called Chum Bucket, which is near my restaurant.

His only goal is stealing the Krubby Patty secret formula and running me out of business, a desire that has led him to destroy the Krusty Krab on more than one occasion. Plankton is egotistical, aggressive and power-hungry. He is very jealous of me and my success. He also has an obsession with "world domination" as he wants to rule the world when he steals the Krubby Patty formula and becomes more successful. He has a minuscule size and overcompensates by being evil and manipulative. He has tried everything from constructing an evil robot to hiring an assassin to kill SpongeBob and Patrick, but all his plans end up failing in the long run.

Plankton's restaurant, the Chum Bucket, has always been extremely unsuccessful, not only because of competition from my restaurant Krusty Krab and Plankton's rude and cruel treatment of the people in Bikini Bottom but also due to the extremely poor quality of the food and Plankton's constant failure to clean the place up. As its name implies, the Chum Bucket serves chum-based foods, all of which tastes terrible.

Youth

In the early 1960s, I joined the Navy. I earned the nickname "Armor Abs Krabs," due to being the manliest of the crew. My shipmates were Torpedo Belly, Mutton Chop, Iron Eye, and Lockjaw Jones. I was apparently also an officer in the navy, for he was a "naval cadet". Having served in the Navy, I know how to defend myself and am more armed than most people.

During this time, my cooking skill improved, and towards the end of my career as a Naval officer, I found myself on a Naval cargo ship, which was carrying suntan lotion. The crew loved me cooking, but My superior, Captain Scarfish, did not like that me and my team not having slop. One day, the cargo ship was invaded by pirates. Me along with Captain Scarfish and the SpongeBob guard, captured the pirates. Then I freed the pirate leader, the pirate queen, and joined her as a pirate.
